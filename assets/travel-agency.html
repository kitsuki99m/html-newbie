Top Itineraries

Here are three of the top spots that tourists and locals can recommend visiting for starters.

El Nido

El Nido, located on the northern tip of Palawan, Philippines, is a premier tropical destination renowned for its dramatic limestone karst cliffs, hidden lagoons, and crystal-clear turquoise waters. As part of a managed resource protected area with biodiversity similar to Borneo, it offers world-class island hopping, white-sand beaches like Nacpan, and stunning, 180-degree ocean views, often accessed by boat or canopy walks

Coron

Coron, located in northern Palawan, Philippines, is a premier tropical destination renowned for its dramatic limestone karst cliffs, crystal-clear turquoise lakes, and world-class WWII shipwreck diving. As part of the Calamian Archipelago, it features iconic sites like Kayangan Lake, Twin Lagoon, and diverse marine life, making it a top spot for snorkeling, diving, and island-hopping.

Underground River

An underground river, specifically referencing the famed Puerto Princesa Subterranean River, is a 8.2 km-long waterway flowing entirely beneath the Saint Paul Mountain Range in Palawan, Philippines, directly into the sea. It features a complex limestone cave system with massive chambers, stalactites, and stalagmites, representing a unique mountain-to-sea ecosystem.
